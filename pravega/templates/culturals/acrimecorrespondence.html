Description

A Crime Correspondence is Pravega's two-round murder mystery event. Put your analytical skills into use to piece the events that finally lead to a gruesome murder together. Will you be quick enough with your deduction to catch the killer before it is too late? Find out at A Crime Correspondence, here at Pravega. The first round (prelims) will be online, and the second round (finals) will be offline at IISc, Bangalore.

Prizes

Worth 1k for Prelims and worth 15k for the finals!


Venue:

  • Prelims: Online
  • Finals: Old Physics Building, IISc Bangalore


Eligibility

Anyone and everyone can take part in the prelims. Teams of up to 3. The top finishers of prelims will move on to the finals.
