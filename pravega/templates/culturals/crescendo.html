Description

Pravega brings to you a magnificent platform to showcase your musical talent! It features two categories: Cabaletta (Solo singing) and Candenza(Solo instrumental). Crescendo starts off with online qualifiers which requires you to submit a 3–4-minute video performing a musical piece. This will be followed by offline finals comprising of two fun-filled rounds which will take place in the IISc campus on the 2nd of September.
