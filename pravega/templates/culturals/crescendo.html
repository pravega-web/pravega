Description

Pravega brings to you a magnificent platform to showcase your musical talent! It features two categories: Cabaletta (Solo singing) and Candenza(Solo instrumental). Crescendo starts off with online qualifiers which requires you to submit a 3–4-minute video performing a musical piece. This will be followed by offline finals comprising of two fun-filled rounds which will take place in the IISc campus on the 2nd of September. Come aboard and join us in this journey of discovering the music within you. Hope to see you soon!

Eligibility

Open to all age groups between 14 to 30 years of age.

Venue

Qualifiers will be held online. The Finals will be held in the IISc Campus!

Prizes

The prize pool is worth ₹20k+, consisting of Cash prizes, exciting trophies and more!
