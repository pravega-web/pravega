Footprints

Description

Footprints [Nukkad Natak] are the prints you make on society by making awareness about an issue through your street play footwork within a crowd. It is a Stage-Play contest with a prize pool of cash totaling ₹ 20000 and certificates that will be awarded to the winners decided based on public choice as well as the Judges Panel (constituted by Pravega). The event is in 2 stages: Participants are required to send their videos to us on our mail id and the top 7 selected teams will get the opportunity to perform live in IISc for the final showdown.

Eligibility

The competition is open to All Institutes (undergrads and postgrads and high school students).

Video Requirements:

  1. The video needs to be sent to our official mail id pravegaproscenium@gmail.com
  2. The performance needs to be in the format of a stage play as if being performed in a theatre with lights and music.
  3. Duration of video can be of max 15 min, although a 9-10 min video is encouraged.
  4. The camera angle should be fixed throughout the video.
  5. Editing the original video is not encouraged.

Judging Criteria:

The videos will be posted in our YouTube and Instagram handles -

  1. The Final scoring will constitute 75% from Judges and 25% from the popularity gained (Likes, shares, comments by the audience) by the post on YouTube.
  2. Following General Benchmarks will be used in the evaluation by the judges:
    • The impact of the issue addressed by the video
    • The direction of the content/ story chosen.
    • The creativity of Presentation.
    • Acting Skillset of the participants.
    • Continuity of the merge.

Details for the final round

The final (and offline) round details will be intimated to the selected participants later. The rules and modalities will be discussed individually and posted on the website.

Prizes

  1. Top 3 Finalists will receive prizes worth ₹ 20k.
    • 1st Prize: 9,000
    • 2nd Prize: 6,500
    • 3rd Prize: 4,500
  2. Every finalist will receive a certificate of merit and a gift hamper.
  3. Every participant will receive an e-certificate for participation.
  4. Each team will get a shoutout through our social media handles and the winners will get an exclusive mention from Pravega.
