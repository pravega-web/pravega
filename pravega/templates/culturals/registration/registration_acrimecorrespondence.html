A Crime Correspondence - Registration for Prelims

A Crime Correspondence is the murder mystery event of Pravega. Registrations for prelims will be open from 18th July '22 to 31st July '22. Prelims will be completely online. Participants are requested to go through the brochure.

Registration Guidelines:

  1. Anyone and everyone can take part in the prelims.
  2. Participants can register in teams of up to 3. Only one of the team members will have to register for the whole team.
  3. There is no registration cost. There might be a cap on the number of registrations due to logistics considerations. The registrations will close as soon as the number of registrations reaches that cap.
