Description

Good journalism, succinct and precise accounts of the events that transpired and not the what they are blown out to be has become a rarity in this era of the information overflow. News delivered well is no longer a mere convenience, but an invaluable asset that is being sought by people the world over. It's not a skill that's easy to come by. Pravega, the IISc UG fest, is here with 'Untanglement', a novel event seeking and celebrating the art of delivering news well. If you have ever felt that you know what it takes to up the game, understand the difference between good journalism and mere reporting, here's your chance to participate in a unique experience where you will not only get to chisel your journalistic flair but also meet similar minded people and veterans of the field who'll bring their enthusiasm and deep insight to the table. We can vouch that it would be a wonderful experience for the amateurs and experts alike. So pull out those journals, participate, report and stand a chance to win prizes all while doing an activity that you love!Come, walk a journalistic journey with us! You do not need to have any previous experience in journalism for it, all you need is a receptive mind to see things in a different and critical way, and a willingness to open yourself up to whatever is going around you. Are you up for it?
