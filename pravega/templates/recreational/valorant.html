Rules and Guidelines

Eligibility

  • Any team whose all players have a competitive rank in Valorant is eligible to participate.

Registration

  • The entire team must register by August 13th at 11:59 pm IST by filling out the attached form.
  • There is no registration fee.
  • 5 player registration is necessary in one form, if the team wishes then it can have a sixth registered player as a substitute (not coach). Players can find teammates on our discord server if they do not have a 5-player team
  • Registrations will be capped to the first 64 teams.
  • If you wish to continue form filling sometime later, it is must to pause the form by clicking pause button under current section. Otherwise, the draft of your form will not be saved.
THE PLAYERS MUST JOIN OUR DISCORD SERVER DURING THE TOURNAMENT, AS ALL THE LOGISTICAL INFORMATION WILL BE SHARED THERE ONLY

Rules

  • It is a standard 5v5 overtime-enabled match.
  • We shall have moderators overseeing each match, so any attempt to cheat will lead to disqualification.
  • Any toxicity directed towards either one’s teammates or opponents will not be tolerated and can lead to disqualification according to moderators’ discretion.
  • Ensure that the details provided in the form are correct, including case sensitivity in one’s Riot IDs. Outliers will be kicked from the tournament.
  • All arrangements regarding network connection and gaming hardware would be the sole responsibility of the participants.
  • Any requests for match pause other than timeout (e.g., due to afk teammates) may or may not be entertained at moderators’ discretion and may even lead to the continuation of unbalanced matches (e.g., 4v5).
  • After 15 mins of scheduled match time, if all five team players cannot show up in the moderator’s lobby, then a loss would be considered for that team in the match

Prizes

  • After the Finals, cash prizes will be awarded to the top three teams as follows:
  • 1st place - ₹ 20,000
  • 2nd place - ₹ 12,000
  • 3rd place - ₹ 8,000
