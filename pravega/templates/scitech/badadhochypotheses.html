Description

BAH involves participants coming up with an ad hoc hypotheses which is very evidently bad but presented to an audience in a logically consistent manner. This year would be the first offline edition of BAH and will involve participants presenting their ad hoc hypotheses before an audience followed by rebuttals. In the online preliminary round participants are required to provide a teaser video and a presentation advertising their hypothesis. Participants with the most interesting hypothesis will then be invited to IISc for the offline finals. For more details, refer to the brochure(available in a few days)

Registration fees: Rs.100/-
