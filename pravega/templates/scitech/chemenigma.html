Event Description

Chemenigma, an event designed to test your calibre in chemistry through stimulating questions that demand knowledge coupled with creativity. Enjoy yourselves as you compete with the most brilliant minds of the country while displaying your skill and ingenuity in solving problems.

The Final event will truly bring out the Enigma of Chemenigma. The finalists would have to work their way through challenging problems in India's finest research labs with the exciting opportunity to present and defend their work in front of leading Chemists in the panel of judges!

Do you have what it takes to be in the Finals?
