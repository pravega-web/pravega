Description

Welcome to this one of a kind contest, a rare event among college fests. Exhibitions is a part of the science and technology events of Pravega 2022. The aim of the event is to poke your curiosity in science and technology through various exhibits and demonstrations. IISc Exhibitions is a unique initiative to bring out the hidden scientists in all of us. Candidates are invited to present working exhibits, and/or simulations to solve a novel/real world problem through holistic approach.


Structure of event

  • Participants will be required to present their exhibits in a live online session on 13th or the 14th August, where they would have to explain the working and how their model solves a novel/real world problem.
  • The top 5 teams would be selected, based on, but not limited to, their idea, innovation and presentation.
  • The selected teams will get an opportunity to showcase their exhibits at the IISc campus during Pravega. The details of this will be informed in due time.

Eligibility

  • Interested students from High School to 3rd year Undergraduate.
  • Registration as a team is mandatory. Individual entries are not allowed. Teams should consist of at most 3 students. Cross institution teams are allowed.
  • Each team member should possess a valid student ID card, issued by their institutions.

Prizes

  • Prizes to be awarded from a pool of 20k
  • Certificates of participation would be provided to all participants.
  • Certificates of merit would be provided to the top 5 teams indicating the position secured


Miscellaneous

  • The participants are required to abide by the above rules and regulations. They will be informed of any changes made in the rules and regulations.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change the rules and regulations at any point of time. In case of any conflict, the organizer's decision is final and binding.
